Scooter Rental Terms and Conditions

1. Reservations and Booking

1.1. Reservations can be made online, by phone, or in person at any of our rental locations.

1.2. A valid credit card is required to secure your reservation.

1.3. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons.

1.4. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time to avoid cancellation fees.

2. Requirements for Rental

2.1. Renters must be at least 18 years of age.

2.2. A valid driver's license with motorcycle/scooter endorsement is required.

2.3. International customers must provide a valid passport along with their driver's license.

2.4. A security deposit will be required at the time of rental, refundable upon return of the scooter in the same condition.

3. Pricing and Payment

3.1. Our pricing is structured based on scooter categories and rental duration:

  • Category A (Sym Orbit 125cc): 1 day: R400, 2-10 Days: R300/day, 11-29 days: R225/day, 30+ days: R120/day
  • Category B (Jet 14 200cc): 1 day: R450, 2-10 Days: R350/day, 11-29 days: R255/day, 30+ days: R150/day
  • Category C (Citycom 300cc): 1 day: R550, 2-10 Days: R500/day, 11-29 days: R350/day, 30+ days: R250/day
  • Category D (Vespa 150 or 300cc): 1 day: R850, 2-10 Days: R600/day, 11-29 days: R400/day, 30+ days: R250/day

3.2. Full payment is due at the time of pickup.

3.3. We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash payments.

3.4. Additional charges may apply for optional equipment, late returns, or damage to the scooter.

4. Insurance and Liability

4.1. Basic insurance coverage is included in the rental price, covering third-party liability.

4.2. Additional comprehensive insurance is available for purchase, which reduces your liability in case of damage or theft.

4.3. The renter is responsible for any damage to the scooter not covered by the insurance policy.

4.4. In case of an accident, the renter must notify us immediately and file a police report when required by law.

5. Pickup and Return

5.1. Scooters must be picked up and returned during our operating hours.

5.2. Late returns will incur additional charges at the hourly rate of the rented scooter category.

5.3. The scooter must be returned with the same fuel level as at pickup.

5.4. A fuel surcharge will apply if the scooter is returned with less fuel than at pickup.

6. Usage Restrictions

6.1. Scooters must not be taken off-road or used for racing or stunt riding.

6.2. Scooters must not be operated by anyone other than the authorized renter or additional drivers listed on the rental agreement.

6.3. Scooters must not be operated under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any substances that may impair driving ability.

6.4. Violation of usage restrictions may result in immediate termination of the rental agreement and additional charges.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our rental terms and conditions, please contact us.

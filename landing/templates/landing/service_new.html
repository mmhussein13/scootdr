Maintenance Tips

Keep your scooter running smoothly between service visits with these essential maintenance tips.

Check tire pressure weekly. Proper inflation ensures good handling, fuel efficiency, and tire longevity. Look for cuts, embedded objects, and irregular wear patterns. Replace tires when the tread depth is below 1.5mm.

Check engine oil level regularly, preferably before each ride. The oil should be between the minimum and maximum marks on the dipstick. Also check brake fluid levels and ensure there are no leaks in the system.

Keep battery terminals clean and free of corrosion. For non-sealed batteries, check electrolyte levels monthly and top up with distilled water if needed. If your scooter sits unused for extended periods, consider using a battery tender.

Scooter Maintenance Tips

Regular Oil Changes

Change your engine oil every 1000-2000km or as recommended by the manufacturer. Fresh oil ensures proper lubrication and extends engine life.

Tire Pressure

Check tire pressure weekly. Proper inflation improves handling, fuel efficiency, and tire longevity. The recommended pressure is usually listed on the tire wall.

Battery Care

Keep battery terminals clean and check voltage regularly. Consider a trickle charger for long storage periods to maintain battery health.
